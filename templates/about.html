Our Mission

We are a group of students working to connect hospitals in the Virginia-DC-Maryland region with desperately needed supplies in the wake of the COVID-19 pandemic. We are also working on independently producing hospital-approved masks and other supplies, with the eventual goal to organize households across the DMV into a production center for masks and other materials to assist healthcare workers.
